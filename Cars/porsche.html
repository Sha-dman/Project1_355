2019 Porsche 911 GT3RS

The Porsche 911 GT3 RS is a track-focused sports car, boasting a lightweight build, aerodynamic enhancements, and a potent naturally aspirated flat-six engine. With precision-tuned suspension and race-inspired design, it delivers thrilling performance and exceptional agility, making it a formidable competitor on both road and track circuits.
  • Intelligent All-Wheel Drive: Provides exceptional traction and stability on any road surface.
  • Naturally aspirated flat-six engine: Provides high-revving power and a distinctive exhaust note.
  • Rear-wheel drive: Enhances agility and responsiveness, particularly on the track.
  • Seven-speed dual-clutch transmission: Enables quick and seamless gear changes for optimal performance.
  • Variable valve timing and lift: Enhances engine efficiency and responsiveness across different driving conditions.
